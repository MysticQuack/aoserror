## Details
- Provider: Default
- Type: Fatal
- Applies to versions: 0.1.2ddtar+

## Cause of error
The files required to start the AliceOS integrity check was missing.

## Possible solutions
- Check the AliceOS installations files.
- Reinstall AliceOS.
